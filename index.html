This past year I listened to just over 1,000 new-to-me albums in the span of 47 weeks. It was a constant push to listen to ~22 new albums each week. I learned so much about the process of picking up on subtle patterns that certain genres share. One of my big take aways is the more of a genre/style of music I listen to, the more I will appreciate and enjoy it as time goes on. As my favorite album list shows, I naturally gravitate towards rap/hip-hop, however I can cofidently say I enjoyed ~95% of all the albums I listened to!
